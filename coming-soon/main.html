About COVU

Building Nigeria's most trusted marketplace where fashion entrepreneurs thrive

Our Story

COVU was born from the collective experiences of our team, who witnessed countless Nigerians struggle with online scams and unfair marketplace practices.

We saw hardworking fashion entrepreneurs selling clothes, bags, shoes, wigs, and perfumes being overlooked by platforms that favored big businesses.

Together, we decided to build a marketplace that levels the playing field, ensuring every seller gets fair visibility and every buyer can shop with confidence.

Our Vision

We aim to create a safe environment for everyone to buy and sell securely, empower young businesses to meet more buyers, and make it easy for anyone to become a seller fast and hassle-free.

We want to make online commerce in Nigeria honest, transparent, and accessible to all.
